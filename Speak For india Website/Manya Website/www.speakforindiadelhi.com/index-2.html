Speak for India

Speak for India is an initiative by Federal Bank in association with The Hindustan Times Group to encourage young, dynamic and enthusiastic minds to raise their voice on pertinent issues and play the role of change maker in society.

The event offers college students a podium to explore their awareness, insights and perspectives on contemporary topics.

How can I Join

You can join by registering a form from this site. The registration is only for limited time. Before registering you have to read the terms and conditions and FAQ to get better understanding about the program. The registration is only for college students who are studying in DELHI NCR. FAQs

Most frequently questions and answers

How can I participate in the competition?

Registrations for Speak For India (Delhi Edition) are now closed. Please try again next year

Who can participate in the competition?

The competition is open for all college students who are currently studying in any of the colleges across Delhi NCR

How do I understand whether I’m selected or not?

All selected candidates will get a confirmation mail from mail@speakforindiadelhi.com with all relevant details about the competition.

Is it a team or individual competition?

Speak for India is an individual debate competition.

When does registration close?

The registration closes on 10th December, 2019 at 11:59:59 pm

What is the language medium of the competition?

The contestants can choose their preferred language among English, Hindi, Punjabi and Haryanavi for the Delhi Edition.
